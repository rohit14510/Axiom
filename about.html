About us

Axiom Craft is a premier name in the interior furnishing and refurbishing industry, specializing in delivering high-quality components and end-to-end services for Indian Railway coaches. With a strong focus on innovation, craftsmanship, and precision, we have positioned ourselves as a trusted partner in transforming railway interiors to meet modern standards of comfort and functionality.

WE ARE THE

Leaders in Interior Furnishing of Coaches Since 2000

Established in the year 2000, at Gurgaon (Haryana, India), we "Shiv Shakti Body Repairing Works", are identified as the noticeable Manufacturer of a wide assortment of Carrier Truck Body, Truck Body, Container Body, Motor Cycle and Scooter Body, Car Carrier, Platform, Defense Vehicle Body, Emergency Rescue Vehicle Body, Fire Vehicle Body, Mobile Van Body, Shipping Container, Security Van Body, Tata Ace Container Body, Pickup Container Body, Hydraulic Body, Food Van Body. We are a Sole Proprietorship Company moving ahead towards success with the support of our adroit experts in this domain. Our offered products are manufactured using the best quality raw materials and extremely progressive technology, under the direction of skilled professionals. The company works in two different models: refurbishment of old coaches and interior furnishing of new coaches. In the refurbishment model, we completely restructure the existing coaches (Conventional type NON LHB), while in the furnishing model we do the design entire coach interior and furnish the LHB coaches with the most advanced material and technology in production units namely MCF and ICF.
